BTS Bangtan Boys

Jungkook    Jimin    V    Suga    RM    Jin    J-Hope   

BTS is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The band consists of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, who co-write or co-produce much of their material. Originally a hip hop group, they expanded their musical style to incorporate a wide range of genres, while their lyrics have focused on subjects including mental health, the troubles of school-age youth and coming of age, loss, the journey towards self-love, individualism, and the consequences of fame and recognition.

Jon Jungkook

Jon Jungkook is a South Korean singer and songwriter. He rose to prominence as a member and vocalist of South Korean boy band BTS. He has performed three solo songs as part of BTS' discography— "Begin" in 2016, "Euphoria" in 2018, and "My Time" in 2020—all of which charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart. He also recorded the soundtrack song "Stay Alive" for the BTS-based webtoon 7Fates: Chakho. He was chosen to sing "Dreamers" for the FIFA World Cup in Qatar because of his immense global popularity and the potential to reach a massive audience through the event.

Jimin

Park Ji-min, known as Jimin, also is part of BTS. He is a singer, songwriter and dancer. He has released three solo tracks under BTS'name which are Lie, Serendipity, and Filter. His first credited solo song is the digital track Promise. In 2018, he recorded a duet With You with Ha Sung-woon for the soundtrack of the K-drama Our Blues. In 2023, Jimin released his debut solo album Face which debuted at number one in South Korea and Japan. He recently released his second solo album Muse and its lead single Who which debuted at number one on the Billboard Global 200

Jin

Kim Seok-jin known professionally as Jin is a singer, songwriter who became part of the band in 2013. He has co-written and released three solo tracks with the band such as Awake in 2016, Epiphany in 2018, and Moon in 2020. Apart of singing, he has appeared as a host of multiple South Korean music programs from 2016 to 2018. Later on 2018, he was awared the fifth-class Hwagwan Order of Cultural Merit by the President of South Korea along with the rest of her bandmates due to the contributions to Korean culture.

J-Hope

Jun Ho-seok well known as J-Hope become a BTS member om 2013. He is also known as a South Korean rapper, singer-songwriter, dancer, and record producer. He released his first solo mixtape Hope World in 2018 which peaked number 38 in the U.S. Billboard 200. He became the first BTS member to enter the Billboard Hot 100 as a soloist in 2019 with his single Chicken Noodle Soup, featuring singer Becky G.
